About Me

My name is Zach Kelley. I am currently a student at Brigham Young University, studying Technology and Engineering. I have one year left of school! I currently work for the Harold B. Lee Library as a UX/UI Designer.

I love being creative and working with my hands. Whether it be graphic design, photography, or just tinkering and building things for fun, I love making things. I have a knack for designing simple and intuitive apps and websites, which helps me excel in my job.
